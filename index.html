Site Intent

Miamiblatdr.com is a local Miami boat service and repair shop. They offer outboard repair, parts and trailers for boats. Their website demonstrates what their shop offers to the general public, which is mainly marine equipment and parts. A strong part of their background relies on transmission rebuilding and engine overhauls. Since this is a giant market in Miami, the competition is large and their website plays a big role in gettin clients from all over Florida, targeting the local South Florida market.

Goals for Redesign
  • New modern layout and design with business color scheme
  • New navigation menu with industry based names for page titles
  • New Home page layout that will be present the business services and products
  • New Service and Products page that lists them
  • Responsive design that is mobile device ready

The target audience consists of mostly local residential clients in the south Florida region. A large part of the audience consists of those looking for service and repair for their boat or outboard engine.
